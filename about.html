About Us

ImagePDF Toolkit is a modern, privacy-first suite of image and PDF tools. Our mission is to make file conversion, compression, and editing fast, secure, and accessible for everyone—without uploading your files anywhere.

  • Client-Side Only: All processing is done in your browser for maximum privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is public and transparent on GitHub.
  • Made by Tech U: We are passionate about privacy-first, user-friendly tools for everyone.
