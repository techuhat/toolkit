Scan to PDF Cleanup Guide

Scanned docs ko readable aur professional banana tough lag sakta hai, lekin kuch focused steps se yeh kaafi easy ho jata hai.

Fixes

  • Deskew
  • De‑noise
  • Contrast/levels

Step‑by‑step workflow

  1. Crop & deskew: page borders align
  2. Denoise: light; text ko mushy na banaye
  3. Levels/contrast: background off‑white, text deep black
  4. Optional binarize: pure B/W for OCR clarity
  5. Sharpen: very mild for text

OCR and searchability

OCR layer add karke document searchable aur accessible banta hai. Sensitive data me local OCR preferable.

Try it

PDF Convert me images export karke clean karein ya direct PDF tools me enhance karein. Split/merge ke saath final pack banayein.

Wrap‑up

Legible scans inspire confidence. Clean‑up routine standardize kar dein to har batch consistent banega.
