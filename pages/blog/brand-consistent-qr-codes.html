Brand-Consistent QR Codes

QR ko brand feel dena possible hai — bas readability compromise na ho. Yeh sweet‑spot achieve karne ke simple rules neeche diye gaye hain.

Design rules

  • High contrast colors (dark on light)
  • Quiet zone intact (4 modules minimum)
  • Logo area 15–30% max

Sizing & placement

  • Posters: arm’s length scan ke liye ~3–4 cm
  • Packaging: curved surfaces pe distortion avoid
  • Digital: clutter se door, strong contrast background

Error correction & testing

Higher error correction (M/H) logo ke saath helpful hota hai, lekin data density ko balance karein. 3–4 popular devices pe real‑world test karein (low light, glare).

UTM + destinations

Always track with UTM. Destination page fast aur mobile‑first ho; clear CTA den. Slow pages = wasted scans.

Build it (ImageToolkit Pro)

  1. Open QR Code Studio
  2. Contrast‑safe colors choose karein
  3. Logo add karein; quiet zone intact rahe
  4. Error correction set karein; device tests karein
  5. Export SVG for print, PNG/SVG for digital

FAQs

Q: Logo ke saath scan issues?
A: Error correction M/H, logo size 15–30% tak, aur strong contrast fix karta hai.

Q: Best format?
A: Print ke liye SVG/PDF; digital ke liye PNG/SVG. JPEG blur risk.

Wrap‑up

Brand‑first, scan‑first — dono ek saath possible hai. Contrast + quiet zone + testing se reliability lock ho jaati hai.
