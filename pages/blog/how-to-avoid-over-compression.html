Avoid Over-Compression

Compression ka goal size kam karna hai, quality khatam karna nahi. Visual checks se aap sweet spot jaldi pakad lenge. Yeh playbook practical fixes deta hai.

Watch for

  • Banding (gradients me steps)
  • Halos (over‑sharpening ki wajah se)
  • Color shifts (wrong profiles)

Common causes

  • Over‑aggressive quality (AVIF/WebP)
  • Excessive denoise/sharpen sequence
  • Chroma subsampling on text/UI

Fix playbook

  1. Artifacts spot karein at 100%/200%
  2. Quality +3 to +5 step up
  3. Sharpen amount/radius reduce
  4. UI assets → lossless try karein
  5. sRGB enforce; re‑export with embedded profile

Gradient banding hacks

Mild noise/dither gradients me banding hide karta hai — specially hero backgrounds me. AVIF Q+3 aur light dither combo reliable hai.

Skin tones and brand colors

Human perception most sensitive yahin hota hai. Reference swatches compare karein; agar break ho to quality thoda raise karein ya codec switch (WebP ↔ AVIF) try karein.

Checklist (copy‑paste)

  • No halos on edges
  • No crushed blacks or blown highlights
  • Gradients smooth (no steps)
  • Text/UI edges razor‑sharp
  • CLS‑safe width/height attributes

FAQs

Q: Kya 100 KB per image target sahi hai?
A: Context dependent. Thumbnails 80–120 KB; content 200–420 KB typical. Template wise budget define karein.

Q: WebP ya AVIF me kaun zyada artifacts deta hai?
A: Scenario based. AVIF gradients me better; noisy textures me dono ka behavior test karein.

Wrap‑up

Light files, crisp visuals — balance hi win hai. Over‑optimize mat karein; consistent budgets + visual QA best outcomes dete hain.
